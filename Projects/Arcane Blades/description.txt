Arcane Blades
---
Arcane Blades is a pair of fantasy weapon assets designed for real-time rendering. The project explores contrasting themes between a corrupted organic blade and an arcane energy sword, with a focus on silhouette clarity, material readability, and game-ready topology.
---
Substance Painter, 3DCoat, Photoshop, Marmoset Toolbag, Blender, ZBrush, Maya
