Pumpkin Monster
---
Pumpkin Monster is a stylized character created through a full asset pipeline, including high-poly sculpting in ZBrush, retopology, UVs, baking, hand-painted texturing, and basic rigging with a short looping animation. The project focuses on strong silhouette, expressive forms, clean topology, and maintaining readable stylized materials suitable for real-time use.
---
Substance Painter, 3DCoat, Photoshop, Marmoset Toolbag, Blender, ZBrush
